About AcroniX

AcroniX, $NIX, is a token launched on the MultiversX blockchain with the native currency $EGLD. It aims for sustainable growth and value maintenance, along with security and transparency.

$NIX has simple, definitive tokenomics that respect the above properties. It has a max supply of 9137 tokens, forever. It cannot be minted, burned, frozen, or wiped. It's written in the code. Transparency is accessible to all, with all actions related to the token verifiable through the MultiversX Blockchain Explorer.

Tokenomics & Allocations

  • 25% Initial Liquidity (xExchange Pool)
  • 45% Future Liquidity Injections
  • 15% Treasury Reserves (Partnerships, integrations etc…)
  • 10% Team Allocation
  • 5% Initial Investors

Initial liquidity has been explained. Future liquidity injections: These 45% will be allocated to a locked account that operates strictly with community consent, allowing free voting on important project decisions.

Treasury, team allocation, and initial investors are self-explanatory by title.

Listing: The token will be initially listed in a pool between EGLD and NIX on xExchange, with 20% of the total supply as initial liquidity, at a price determined then. Shortly after listing, it will be available on other DEXs in the ecosystem like Onedex and Jexchange, and integrated into the xPortal app.
