About AcroniX

AcroniX is a long-term project built on MultiversX, designed to ride the waves of growth with scarcity and utility at its core. Unlike memecoins, AcroniX fosters a stable community of holders and supports developers by providing a hub to showcase innovative applications. List your software on the AcroniX Hub (in development), where the best projects will be featured. With 2% of the token supply dedicated to rewarding top projects, AcroniX is the platform that empowers other platforms—building a thriving ecosystem for creators and investors alike.
